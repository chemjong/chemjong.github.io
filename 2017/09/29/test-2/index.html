The density plot shows that people who leave have low level of satisfaction. We can observe that most of the satisfaction level for leaving are less that .50 We can claim that anything above 0.75 is pretty good.

Based on above claims let run correlation matrix for all data above .75 satisfaction level

data1 %>% 
        filter(satisfaction_level > .75) %>% 
        select(1:8) %>% 
        map_df(~as.numeric(.)) %>%

Above cor plot indicates that employees with high satisfaction may leave due to working on many projects, spending many hours in work and not being promoted.
